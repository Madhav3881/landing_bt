Industry Defined Problems

Industry defined problem is where the participants get introduced to industrial problems and come up with innovative solutions. We are looking for ideas and concepts that are creative and elegant solutions to seemingly complex engineering problems. It is an event to collaborate academic brilliance to real life industrial problem to put imagination to work. This is an sponsored event and also a chance for the company to find the solution to the problem from young minds across the nation. The solution provided by the participants in previous years are now being implemented in actuality.
